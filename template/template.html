Informational Model to Star Schema


This decisional tool converts an informational model consisting of information such as facts, categories, attributes, computed from data to a well-structured star schema with links between facts, dimensions and their attributes. The star schema is the simplest type of Data Warehouse Schema. It is known as a star schema because its structure resembles the shape of a star. In the star schema, the centre contains a fact table and the pointed corners denote the dimensions of the informational model.
